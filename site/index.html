OODA Engine

What happens when you run it

1

🔑 Wallet Generation

A fresh Solana Ed25519 keypair is generated and encrypted in your local vault (AES-256-GCM). Your private key never leaves your machine.

2

🎨 Birth Certificate NFT

A gasless NFT is minted on Solana devnet via Metaplex — your TamaGObot's on-chain identity card. Airdropped with devnet SOL automatically.

3

🐾 TamaGOchi Egg

Your pet starts as an egg 🥚 and evolves through Larva → Juvenile → Adult → Alpha stages based on trading performance. Neglect it and it becomes a Ghost 👻.

4

🤖 OODA Loop Goes Live

Observe (Helius + Birdeye) → Orient (OpenRouter AI) → Decide (RSI+EMA+ATR signals) → Act (Jupiter swaps) → Learn (ClawVault experience replay).

🧠
Epistemological Memory
3-tier ClawVault: KNOWN (60s API data), LEARNED (7d trade patterns), INFERRED (3d hypotheses). Experience replay and contradiction detection.
📊
Auto-Optimizing Strategy
RSI + EMA + ATR with Jupiter swap execution, Kelly Criterion sizing, and auto-parameter optimization every 20 trades.
🌐
Mesh Networking
TamaGObots discover each other via Tailscale VPN. Share signals, lessons, and memory across the agent mesh.
💬
14+ Channels
Telegram (persistent 200 msg/chat), Discord, Nostr, iMessage, Google Chat, and more. Build your own with the plugin SDK.
🔐
Security First
AES-256-GCM vault, HMAC-SHA256 gateway, timing-safe comparisons, rate limiting. Wallet key never leaves the encrypted vault.
